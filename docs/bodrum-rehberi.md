*   **Fuel:** 2 fast pumps at the marina entrance. 
*   **Limitation:** A maximum of boats up to 40 meters can dock at the fuel pier.
*   **Depth:** Due to being in the city center, the depth in the inner harbor is between 3.5 - 5 meters.

## 3. D-Marin Turgutreis (VHF 72)
*   **Fuel:** Duty-free fuel is available. It is the last provisioning point before the Islands (Kos/Leros) crossing.
